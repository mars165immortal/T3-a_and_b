About Smart Car Control Panel

Smart Car Control Panel is a web application that allows users to remotely monitor and control their smart cars. Users can view information about their car, such as its current speed and battery level, and also control features like the lights and climate control.

This application was created as a demonstration of the capabilities of modern web technologies, and is not affiliated with any particular car manufacturer or smart car platform.
